Pricing Complexity of the Process Industry

Process manufacturing industries face unique challenges when it comes to pricing.

Pricing Challenges

Companies face increasing pricing complexity due to raw material volatility, adoption of indexed and value-based pricing, intricate pricing equations, and misalignment across siloed teams.

Customized Products

In addition to standard and commoditized products—which can themselves be challenging to price—these sectors also deal with customized items adding further complexity to the quoting process, spot or longterm

alt="Operational Impact">

Operational Impact

These challenges lead to time-consuming and error-prone quotations, lost sales, margin erosion, and inefficient workflows that can consume up to 50% of sales resources.

How We Solve It

Pricera eliminates the need for cumbersome spreadsheets, manual pricing rules, and lengthy alignment meetings.

Single Automated Tool

It offers a single, automated SaaS tool that simplifies pricing and quoting processes across plants, divisions, and even countries.

Powerwul pricing engine

Whether used as a standalone solution or integrated with existing CRM/ERP systems, Pricera adapts to the unique sales processes and product complexities of each company.

AI & Machine Learning

Its AI and Machine Learning module leverages collected sales data to optimize pricing strategies and drive continuous improvement.

Benefits

Pricera delivers transformative results for businesses.

Increased Efficiency

By reducing quotation times by over 90%, it shrinks sales cycles from months, weeks, days to just minutes.

Revenue Growth

This efficiency, combined with optimized pricing models, boosts revenue and operating profits.

Policy Compliance

Pricera automates compliance while maintaining complete transparency - the foundation for reliable B2B manufacturing operations.
